Achievements

CES 2018 Best Smartphone Overall

During the CES event in 2018, one of our products was declared 1st place for the category of CES 2018 Best Smartphone Overall. There were 4 other shortlisted nominees who were Apple, Samsung, Huawei, and Google. We, the employees, at GenTech were dedicated and worked very hard to innovate the best smartphone for our customers. We were also thrilled because we are a new and small company to have won this very competitive award..

More Than 20 Million Products Sold!

On December 16th 2018, GenTech managed to sell more than 20 million products. This is a huge milestone for GenTech. We are very proud to have millions of customers who made the choice to use our products that we manufactured. We will continue to innovate more products that will benefit people and their lifestyles..

First Company To Release Bendable Smartphone Prototype!

On September 1st 2018, GenTech was the first ever company to announce a bendable smartphone is in testing phase and 100 prototypes has been released during the CES convention. The prototype was given a year of testing in Japan on September 15th 2018.This smartphone marks GenTech has made a big step in technology field and brings the future nearer.
