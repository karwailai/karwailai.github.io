Events

CES 2019

GenTech will be attending to CES 2019!There will be our own GenTech exhibition area and stage.We will be unveiling new and exciting products this year. We hope to see you there.

When will CES 2019 be held?

June 27th - June 30th 2019

Where will CES 2019 be held?

Las Vegas Convention Center, Las Vegas Valley, Nevada, United States.

What are the visiting hours?

12:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Where can you find GenTech's exhibition?

North side of the Las Vegas Convention Center

To purchase tickets/find out more information about CES 2019 visit ces-2019

Q&A Session

GenTech will be holding a question and answer session this May! We are holding this because we would like to know what you think of our company and your thoughts with our products. However we will not be answering any questions related to the details of our new products. We hope to see you there and express your opinions.

When will the Q&A be held?

May 20th 2019

Where will the Q&A be held?

Kuala Lumpur Convention Centre, Kuala Lumpur City Centre, 50088 Kuala Lumpur, Wilayah Persekutuan Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

What are the visiting hours?

2:00 PM - 6:00 PM

GenTech Sports Competition

GenTech will be holding a sports competition this August! This sports event will be slightly different from the regular sports competitions. Instead of medals, the top 3 winners of each event will win GenTech products.

  • 1st place will win a smartphone, laptop, and smartwatch
  • 2nd place will win a smartphone and smartwatch
  • 3rd place will win a smartwatch

To sign up for the sports competition visit GenTech Sports Competition

We hope to see you compete, have fun and get your game on!
